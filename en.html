Why Choose MedMediaPlayer

Powerful yet easy-to-use media playback solution

All Format Support

Play almost all audio and video formats including MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, FLV, MP3, AAC, and more. No additional codecs needed.

Cross-Platform

Consistent experience on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS with sync for playback progress and media libraries.

Hardware Acceleration

Utilize GPU for hardware decoding to significantly reduce CPU usage and enable smoother 4K/8K video playback.

Security & Privacy

Completely open-source, ad-free, no tracking, and no collection of personal information. Your privacy is respected.

Highly Customizable

Customize skins, interface layouts, and extend functionality with plugins to meet advanced user needs.

Streaming Support

Supports HTTP, RTSP, RTMP, HLS and other streaming protocols. Play online videos and live streams directly.
